About Us

Our mission, our vision, and our story.

Welcome to the School of Engineering at the University of Ghana! At the School of Engineering, our mission is to foster excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation. Established with a vision to be a leading institution in engineering education in Africa, we are dedicated to providing high-quality academic programs and cutting-edge research opportunities.

Our Vision

To be a world-class engineering school renowned for transformative education, impactful research, and innovative solutions that address global and local challenges.

Our Mission

  • Deliver Excellence in Education: We aim to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and competencies required to excel in the ever-evolving field of engineering. Our curriculum is designed to blend theoretical knowledge with practical experience, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared for the demands of the engineering profession.

  • Advance Research and Innovation: Our faculty and students engage in pioneering research that pushes the boundaries of engineering. We are committed to addressing pressing societal issues through innovative solutions and collaborative research.

  • Foster Industry Collaboration: We maintain strong ties with industry leaders and organizations to ensure our programs remain relevant and our students gain real-world experience. Our partnerships with industry stakeholders help bridge the gap between academic learning and practical application.

  • Promote Sustainable Development: We are dedicated to fostering sustainable engineering practices that contribute to the development of our community and the protection of our environment. Our research and projects focus on finding sustainable solutions that benefit society as a whole.
